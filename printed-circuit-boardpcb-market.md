<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are categorized into three main types based on the number of layers they have: Single-Sided Boards, Double-Sided Boards, and Multilayer Circuit Boards. Single-Sided Boards have circuitry on only one side, Double-Sided Boards have circuitry on both sides, and Multilayer Circuit Boards have multiple layers of circuitry sandwiched together. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in terms of complexity, size, and functionality, making them suitable for various applications in different industries.</p></p>
