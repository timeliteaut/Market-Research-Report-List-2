<p><p>Chute-Type Color Sorters and Belt-Type Color Sorters are two main types of color sorters in the market. Chute-Type Color Sorters use gravity to separate materials based on color, whereas Belt-Type Color Sorters use a conveyor belt system to scan and sort items. Chute-Type Color Sorters are typically used for smaller, fragile objects, while Belt-Type Color Sorters are more commonly used for larger, heavier items. Both types are essential for various industries such as food processing, recycling, and mining.</p></p>
