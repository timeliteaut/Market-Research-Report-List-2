There are two main types of digital inhalers on the market: Metered Dose Inhalers (MDI) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPI). MDIs deliver medication in aerosol form, while DPIs deliver medication in a dry powder form. Both types of inhalers are equipped with digital technology that helps users track their medication usage and monitor their respiratory health for better management of their condition.</p></p>
