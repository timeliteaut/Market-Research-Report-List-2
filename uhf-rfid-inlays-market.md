<p><p>UHF RFID inlays are used for tracking and identification purposes in various industries. There are two main types of UHF RFID inlays available in the market: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays are designed for applications where a label with the inlay needs to be attached to a surface, while UHF wet inlays are meant for applications where the inlay needs to be directly embedded into a material. Both types offer high performance and reliability for efficient RFID tracking and tagging solutions.</p></p>
