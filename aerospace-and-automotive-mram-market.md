<p><strong>The Aerospace and Automotive MRAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toggle MRAM</li><li>STT-MRAM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace and Automotive MRAM market comprises two main types of MRAM technologies: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM uses a magnetic tunnel junction to switch data bits, offering high speed and endurance. On the other hand, STT-MRAM uses spin-transfer torque to write data, providing lower power consumption and improved scalability. Both technologies are being utilized in aerospace and automotive applications for their non-volatile, high-speed, and rugged characteristics, catering to the demanding requirements of these industries.</p></p>
