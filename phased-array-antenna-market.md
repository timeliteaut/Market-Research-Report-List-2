<p><p>Phased Array Antenna market consists of two main types, Active Phased Array Antenna and Passive Phased Array Antenna. Active phased array antennas use electronic components for beamforming, enabling fast and precise pointing of the antenna beam. On the other hand, passive phased array antennas depend on phase shifters and power dividers for beamforming, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both types are widely used in applications such as radar systems, satellite communication, and wireless networks for their ability to steer or shape the antenna beam electronically.</p></p>
