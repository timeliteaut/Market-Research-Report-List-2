<p><p>Resolver market can be broadly categorized into two types based on their technology: Brushless Resolvers and Brushed Resolvers. Brushless Resolvers are more advanced and reliable as they do not have any brushes which reduces wear and tear, providing higher accuracy and longer life span. On the other hand, Brushed Resolvers use brushes for commutation, making them more cost-effective but less precise and durable compared to their brushless counterparts. Both types cater to different market segments based on their specific requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
