<p><p>Backing pumps for the semiconductor market include liquid ring pumps, gas-circulation-cooled pumps, screw pumps, and multi-stage roots pumps. Liquid ring pumps use a rotating liquid to create a vacuum, gas-circulation-cooled pumps use gas cooling to achieve low temperatures, screw pumps are ideal for clean applications, and multi-stage roots pumps provide high pumping speeds. Each type of pump has its own advantages and is used in various semiconductor manufacturing processes to ensure efficient and reliable vacuum systems.</p></p>
