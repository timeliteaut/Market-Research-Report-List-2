<p><p>High voltage regulators are essential components in power distribution systems to ensure stable voltage levels. Manual type regulators require manual adjustment of settings to maintain desired voltage levels. On the other hand, automatic type regulators use sensors and control systems to automatically adjust voltage levels, providing a more convenient and efficient solution. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications, with manual type regulators being more cost-effective and simple to use, while automatic type regulators offer advanced features for enhanced control and monitoring of voltage levels.</p></p>
