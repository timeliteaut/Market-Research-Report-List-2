<p><p>RF coaxial cable assemblies are classified into three main types - semi-rigid, semi-flexible, and flexible. Semi-rigid cable assemblies maintain their shape after bending, making them suitable for applications requiring precise routing. Semi-flexible cable assemblies offer a balance between flexibility and stability, allowing for moderate bending without compromising performance. Flexible cable assemblies provide the highest level of flexibility, making them ideal for applications that require frequent movement or bending. Each type caters to specific needs in the RF market, offering different levels of performance and versatility.</p></p>
