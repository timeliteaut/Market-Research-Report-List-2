<p><strong>The MEMS Microphones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Microphones</li><li>Piezoresistive Microphones</li><li>Piezoelectric Microphones</li><li>Optical Microphones</li><li>FET Microphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS microphones are categorized into different types based on how they function. Capacitive microphones use variations in capacitance to detect sound waves. Piezoresistive microphones use changes in resistance due to pressure variations. Piezoelectric microphones convert mechanical vibrations into electrical signals. Optical microphones measure the light intensity variations caused by sound waves. FET microphones utilize field-effect transistors to convert sound waves into electrical signals. Each type of MEMS microphone offers unique advantages and is used in various applications based on their specific capabilities.</p></p>
