<p><p>5G indoor small base stations operate in two main market types: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz technology offers wider coverage and better penetration through walls, making it suitable for indoor deployment in homes, offices, and public spaces. On the other hand, mmWave technology provides ultra-fast speeds but has limited coverage and is more prone to obstructions. It is typically used in high-density urban areas where there is a need for high capacity and low latency. Both market types play a crucial role in providing seamless 5G connectivity indoors.</p></p>
