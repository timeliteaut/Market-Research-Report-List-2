<p><p>The outdoor step sensor market includes two main types: gravity sensors and vibration sensors. Gravity sensors measure the tilt or orientation of an object by detecting changes in gravitational force, making them ideal for applications like inclination sensing and motion detection. Vibration sensors, on the other hand, detect vibrations or oscillations in an object or environment, making them suitable for monitoring movement, structural health, and seismic activity. Both types play a crucial role in various outdoor applications, from sports tracking to industrial monitoring.</p></p>
