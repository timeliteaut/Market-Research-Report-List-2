<p><p>Heat impulse sealers are categorized into two main types: Single Sided Heat Impulse Sealers and Double Sided Heat Impulse Sealers. Single sided sealers use heat to seal one side of a package, while double sided sealers seal both sides simultaneously. Single sided sealers are commonly used for lighter duty packaging needs, while double sided sealers are ideal for heavier duty applications. Both types of sealers offer efficient and effective sealing solutions for various industries including food packaging, medical packaging, and more.</p></p>
