<p><p>Industrial inkjet printers are widely used in various industries for coding, marking, and labeling applications. The two main types of industrial inkjet printers are Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop on Demand (DOD). CIJ printers are known for their high-speed printing and versatility, making them ideal for large production lines. DOD printers, on the other hand, are preferred for their precision and ability to produce high-resolution prints, making them suitable for industries that require detailed and accurate markings.</p></p>
