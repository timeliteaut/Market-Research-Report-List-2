<p><ul><li>Singleturn Encoder</li><li>Multiturn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical Absolute Encoders come in two main types: Singleturn Encoders and Multiturn Encoders. Singleturn Encoders provide position information within one revolution of the shaft, while Multiturn Encoders offer position data for multiple shaft revolutions. Singleturn Encoders are suitable for applications where a full rotation is sufficient, while Multiturn Encoders are ideal for systems that require multiple shaft revolutions to be tracked accurately. Both types offer precise position feedback, making them essential components in various mechanical systems.</p></p>
