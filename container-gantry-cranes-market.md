<p><strong>The Container Gantry Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Main Girder</li><li>Double Main Girder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Container Gantry Cranes are classified into two main types: Single Main Girder and Double Main Girder. Single Main Girder cranes have one main girder supporting the trolley, making them more lightweight and cost-effective for lower capacity applications. Double Main Girder cranes, on the other hand, have two main girders for heavier lifting capacities and greater stability. Both types of cranes are commonly used in container terminals for efficiently transferring containers between ships and the terminal yard.</p></p>
