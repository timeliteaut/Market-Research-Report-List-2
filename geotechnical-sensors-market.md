<p><p>Geotechnical sensors market can be divided into different types based on their specific functions. Extensometers are used to measure deformation and strain in soil or rock. Piezometers monitor water pressures in the ground. Rain gauges measure precipitation levels. Thermometers track temperature changes in the soil. Other sensors may include inclinometers, crack meters, and tilt meters, which provide data on slope stability and ground movements. Each type of sensor plays a crucial role in geotechnical monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
