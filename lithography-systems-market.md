<p><strong>The Lithography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Printers</li><li>Proximity Printers</li><li>Projection Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithography systems market is divided into three main types based on the proximity between the mask and the substrate during the printing process. Contact printers have direct contact between the mask and substrate, proximity printers have a small gap between them, and projection printers use a lens to project the image onto the substrate. Each type has its own advantages and limitations in terms of resolution, throughput, and cost, catering to different needs within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
