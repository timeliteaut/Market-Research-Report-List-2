<p><p>Prefilled flush syringes are available in different types, including saline flush syringes, heparin flush syringes, and other variations. Saline flush syringes are used to flush IV lines to prevent blockages and maintain patency. Heparin flush syringes contain a small amount of heparin to prevent clot formation in IV lines. Other types of prefilled flush syringes may include medications such as antibiotics or anticoagulants. These syringes are convenient and help prevent infections and complications in patients with intravenous access.</p></p>
