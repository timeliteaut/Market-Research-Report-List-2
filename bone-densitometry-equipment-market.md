<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Bone Densitometer</li><li>X-ray Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bone densitometry equipment market is segmented into two main types: ultrasound bone densitometers and X-ray bone densitometers. Ultrasound bone densitometers use sound waves to measure bone density, making them radiation-free and safe for frequent use. On the other hand, X-ray bone densitometers use X-ray technology to measure bone density, providing more detailed and accurate results but exposing patients to low levels of radiation. Both types of equipment have their own advantages and are widely used in diagnosing and monitoring conditions like osteoporosis.</p></p>
