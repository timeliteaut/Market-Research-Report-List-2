<p><p>Slurry valves are commonly available in two main market types: Wafer Type and Full Flanged Type. Wafer Type valves are compact and lightweight, designed to fit between flanges in a pipeline. They are easy to install and remove, making maintenance simpler. Full Flanged Type valves, on the other hand, are robust and sturdy, suitable for high-pressure applications. They are bolted to the pipeline flanges for added security and stability. Both types of valves serve specific purposes and cater to different industry needs.</p></p>
