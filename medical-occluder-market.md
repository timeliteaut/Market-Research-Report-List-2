<p><p>The medical occluder market is segmented into various types based on the application, such as PDA Occluder, ASD Occluder, VSD Occluder, LAA Occluder, and PFO Occluder. PDA, ASD, and VSD occluders are used for closing abnormal connections in the heart, while LAA occluders are used for sealing off the left atrial appendage to prevent blood clots. PFO occluders are used to close a small hole between the upper chambers of the heart. Each type of occluder serves a specific medical purpose in cardiology interventions.</p></p>
