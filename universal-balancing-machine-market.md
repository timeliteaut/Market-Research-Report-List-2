<p><p>Universal Balancing Machine market includes two main types: Vertical Balancing Machine and Horizontal Balancing Machine. Vertical Balancing Machines are specifically designed to work with vertical rotating parts, such as rotors and armatures. They are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics. Horizontal Balancing Machines, on the other hand, are used for balancing rotating parts that operate horizontally, like crankshafts and turbine rotors. They are crucial for ensuring smooth operation and performance of machinery in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
