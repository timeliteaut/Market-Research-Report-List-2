There are two main types of chute samplers: Gravity Chute Samplers and Sloped Gravity Chute Samplers. Gravity chute samplers use gravity to pull the material through the sampling chute, while Sloped Gravity Chute Samplers have an inclined chute that allows for easier sample collection. Both types are used in various industries to ensure accurate and unbiased sampling of materials.</p></p>
