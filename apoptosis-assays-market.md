<p><strong>The Apoptosis Assays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Caspase Assays</li><li>Annexin V and Cell Permeability Assays</li><li>DNA Fragmentation Assays</li><li>Mitochondrial Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apoptosis assays are used to detect and measure different aspects of programmed cell death. Caspase assays measure the activity of enzymes involved in apoptosis. Annexin V and cell permeability assays assess changes in cell membrane integrity. DNA fragmentation assays detect DNA fragmentation, a key feature of apoptosis. Mitochondrial assays evaluate mitochondrial membrane potential, which is altered during apoptosis. Each type of assay provides valuable information about the apoptotic process and is used in research, drug development, and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
