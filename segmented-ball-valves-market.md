<p><p>Segmented ball valves are categorized into two main types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves. Soft seal valves have a seating surface made of resilient materials such as plastic or rubber, providing a tight seal when closed. On the other hand, hard seal valves have a seating surface made of metal or other durable materials, offering a longer lifespan and increased resistance to high temperatures and pressures. Each type of segmented ball valve is suited for different applications based on their sealing capabilities and durability.</p></p>
