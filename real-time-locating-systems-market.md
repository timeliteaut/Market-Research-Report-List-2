<p><p>Real Time Locating Systems (RTLS) use different technologies for precise location tracking. ZigBee is a low-power, short-range wireless communication standard, suitable for tracking objects in small areas like warehouses. Wi-Fi RTLS leverages existing Wi-Fi networks for location accuracy in larger spaces such as hospitals or airports. Ultra Wide Band (UWB) technology offers high accuracy and reliability for tracking assets in real-time, making it ideal for industries requiring precise location data like manufacturing or logistics. Each market type caters to specific needs based on range, accuracy, and power consumption requirements.</p></p>
