<p><p>Ultrasound access needles are used for minimally invasive procedures such as endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle aspiration (EUS-FNA) and endoscopic ultrasound-guided fine needle biopsy (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA needles are primarily used for obtaining tissue samples for cytological analysis, while EUS-FNB needles are used for obtaining tissue samples for histological analysis. Both types of needles are crucial in diagnosing and staging various medical conditions, such as cancer, by providing accurate and precise tissue samples through ultrasound guidance.</p></p>
