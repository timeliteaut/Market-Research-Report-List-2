<p><p>Laminar Airflow Cabinets are used in various industries to provide a clean and sterile working environment. Vertical Laminar Airflow Cabinets are designed to direct air flow vertically down onto the work surface, while Horizontal Laminar Airflow Cabinets have air flow that moves horizontally across the work surface. Both types of cabinets offer protection against contamination and are used in laboratories, medical facilities, and pharmaceutical companies. Vertical cabinets are ideal for tasks requiring minimal air disruption, while horizontal cabinets are suitable for applications needing greater air coverage.</p></p>
