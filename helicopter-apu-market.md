<p><p>Helicopter APU market includes two main types: Electric APU and Pneumatic APU. Electric APUs are powered by electricity and are commonly used in modern helicopters for their efficiency and reliability. They are known for providing consistent power output and are easier to maintain. On the other hand, Pneumatic APUs operate using compressed air and are preferred for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They are commonly found in older helicopter models and are still widely used in the industry. Both types of APUs provide essential auxiliary power for helicopter operations.</p></p>
