<p><ul><li>Round</li><li>Cone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Beam Crucibles are available in two main types: Round and Cone. Round crucibles are cylindrical in shape and are commonly used for holding materials during the e-beam evaporation process. Cone crucibles, on the other hand, have a tapered cone shape which allows for easier loading of materials and can help prevent spillage. Both types of crucibles are commonly used in the semiconductor, optical coating, and research industries for various thin film deposition applications.</p></p>
