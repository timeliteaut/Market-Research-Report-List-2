<p><p>DisplayPort technology has expanded to various market types catering to different needs. Embedded DisplayPort is integrated into devices like laptops and tablets. Wireless DisplayPort allows for wireless streaming of content to external displays. Internal DisplayPort connects components within a device. Each market type serves specific purposes in the realm of audiovisual connectivity.</p></p>
