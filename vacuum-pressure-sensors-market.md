<p><p>Vacuum pressure sensors are used in various industries to measure pressure levels within vacuum systems. Three common types of vacuum pressure sensors include Piezo sensors, which use the piezoelectric effect to measure pressure changes; Pirani sensors, which rely on the thermal conductivity of gases to measure pressure; and CDG sensors, which utilize a convection-driven gauge to determine pressure levels. Each type of sensor has its own unique advantages and applications, making them suitable for different industrial settings and requirements.</p></p>
