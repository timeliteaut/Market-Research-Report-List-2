<p><ul><li>Non-linear</li><li>Linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical couplers are devices used to combine or split optical signals in fiber optic communications systems. Non-linear optical couplers are designed to manipulate the intensity of light signals in a nonlinear way, allowing for more complex signal processing. Linear optical couplers, on the other hand, maintain a linear relationship between input and output signals, making them suitable for simple signal splitting or combining applications. Both types of optical couplers play important roles in various optical communication systems, catering to different signal processing needs.</p></p>
