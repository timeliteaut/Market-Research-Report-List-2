<p><p>Electric rebar benders are available in two main types - semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic rebar benders require some manual effort from the operator to feed and bend the rebar, while fully automatic rebar benders are capable of feeding, bending, and cutting rebar without any manual intervention. The semi-automatic market is suitable for smaller projects where some manual labor is feasible, while the fully automatic market is favored for larger construction projects where efficiency and precision are key.</p></p>
