<p><ul><li>Hydraulic-based</li><li>Actuator-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion simulation market can be divided into two main types: hydraulic-based and actuator-based simulation systems. Hydraulic-based systems use hydraulic fluid to create motion effects, providing a more realistic and immersive experience. Actuator-based systems, on the other hand, use electric actuators to generate motion, offering precise control and flexibility in simulation scenarios. Both types of systems are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, entertainment, and training applications to simulate real-world movements and environments.</p></p>
