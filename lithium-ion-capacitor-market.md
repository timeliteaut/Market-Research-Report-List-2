<p><p>Lithium-ion capacitors are available in two main types: radial type and laminating type. Radial type capacitors have a cylindrical shape, making them easy to install in various electronic devices. On the other hand, laminating type capacitors are flat and flexible, making them suitable for use in thin and compact devices. Both types offer high energy density, quick charging capabilities, and long cycle life, making them popular choices for applications requiring reliable energy storage solutions.</p></p>
