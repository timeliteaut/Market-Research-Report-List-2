<p><p>Induction Holding Furnaces are used for holding and maintaining the temperature of molten metal in foundries and metalworking industries. There are two main types of induction holding furnaces: Coreless Holding Furnaces and Channel Holding Furnaces. Coreless holding furnaces use induction heating to melt and hold metal without a refractory lining, while channel holding furnaces have a refractory-lined channel for holding molten metal. Both types offer efficient and controlled heating for the production of high-quality castings in various industries.</p></p>
