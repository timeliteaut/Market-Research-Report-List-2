<p><strong>The Well Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Downhole Equipment</li><li>Surface Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Well testing equipment market can be categorized into two main types: downhole equipment and surface equipment. Downhole equipment includes tools such as downhole pressure gauges, packers, and flow control valves that are used for measuring and controlling the flow of fluids in the wellbore. Surface equipment, on the other hand, consists of equipment like separators, choke manifolds, and testing units that are used at the surface to process and analyze the fluids that come out of the well during testing operations.</p></p>
