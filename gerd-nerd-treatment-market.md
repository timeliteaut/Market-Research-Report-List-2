<p><p>GERD & NERD treatment market includes antacids (acid neutralizers), proton pump inhibitors, and H2 receptor blockers. Antacids work by neutralizing stomach acid, providing quick relief for mild symptoms. Proton pump inhibitors reduce stomach acid production for long-lasting relief of more severe symptoms. H2 receptor blockers also decrease acid production, but through a different mechanism. Each type of medication targets different aspects of acid reflux to effectively manage symptoms and prevent complications associated with GERD & NERD.</p></p>
