<p><p>Adjustable capacitors are available in various types including Air Gap Adjustable Capacitors, Vacuum Adjustable Capacitors, and SF6 Gas Filled Adjustable Capacitors. Air Gap Adjustable Capacitors feature a variable air gap between its plates for capacitance adjustment. Vacuum Adjustable Capacitors utilize a vacuum seal to adjust capacitance. SF6 Gas Filled Adjustable Capacitors have sulfur hexafluoride gas filled in the capacitor for capacitance adjustment. These different types cater to specific applications and industries where precise capacitance adjustment is required for optimal performance.</p></p>
