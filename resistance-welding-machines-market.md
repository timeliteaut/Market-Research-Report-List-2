<p><p>Resistance welding machines are classified into three main types based on their level of automation: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. </p><p>Manual resistance welding machines require operators to manually adjust and control the welding process. Semi-automatic machines involve some level of automation, with features such as automatic electrode positioning. Finally, automatic resistance welding machines are fully automated, with advanced features like programmable controls and automatic part loading and unloading. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, control, and productivity to suit different welding needs.</p></p>
