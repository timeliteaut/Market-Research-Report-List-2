<p><p>Die casting is a metal casting process that involves forcing molten metal under high pressure into a mold cavity. The mold cavity is created using two hardened steel dies, which have been machined into the desired shape. Once the molten metal solidifies, the dies are opened, and the casting is ejected.</p><p>The die casting market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for die-cast components in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods. Die casting offers several advantages, including high dimensional accuracy, smooth surface finish, and the ability to produce complex shapes with close tolerances.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the die casting market is the adoption of advanced technologies such as computer simulations and robotics to improve the efficiency and quality of the casting process. Additionally, there is a growing trend towards the use of lightweight materials such as aluminum and magnesium alloys in die casting, which helps to reduce the overall weight of the final components.</p><p>Overall, the die casting market is expected to continue growing rapidly in the coming years, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality, complex components in various industries.</p></p>
