<p><p>Roaming tariff market types refer to different categories in which roaming charges are applied for mobile users. Regional roaming refers to roaming within a specific region, national roaming within a country, and international roaming when traveling abroad. Inter-standard roaming involves different technology standards, mobile signature roaming involves authentication services, inter-MSC roaming deals with switching between mobile service providers, permanent roaming allows continuous roaming access, and trombone roaming occurs when international traffic is routed through a different country before reaching its destination.</p></p>
