<p><p>Iron ore pelletizing equipment market includes different types of machines used to convert fine iron ore particles into uniform-sized pellets. Disc pelletizers (disk granulators) utilize a rotating disc to create pellets, while rotary drum pelletizing equipment relies on a rotating drum to form pellets. Both types of equipment are crucial in the pelletizing process, as they help increase the overall efficiency of iron ore pellet production by ensuring a consistent size and shape of the pellets.</p></p>
