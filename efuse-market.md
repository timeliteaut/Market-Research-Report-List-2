<p><ul><li>Latched Type eFuse</li><li>Auto-retry Type eFuse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Latched Type eFuse and Auto-retry Type eFuse are two market segments within the Efuse industry. Latched Type eFuses provide protection by permanently disabling the circuit when overcurrent or overvoltage events occur, ensuring the safety of the system. On the other hand, Auto-retry Type eFuses automatically reset after a fault is detected, allowing for continuous operation without manual intervention. Both types of eFuses offer different levels of protection and functionality to meet the diverse needs of various applications in the electronics market.</p></p>
