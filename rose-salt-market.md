<p><p>Rose salt comes in two main market types: coarse salt and fine salt. Coarse rose salt is typically larger in texture and is often used for cooking and seasoning. It provides a crunchy texture and added flavor to dishes. Fine rose salt, on the other hand, has a smaller grain size and is ideal for baking and blending into recipes where a smoother texture is desired. Both types of rose salt are popular among consumers for their unique flavor profiles and versatility in various culinary applications.</p></p>
