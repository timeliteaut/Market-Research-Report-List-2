<p><strong>The Soil Compactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Rolling Compactor</li><li>Impact Compactor</li><li>Vibrating Compactor</li><li>Compound Action Compactor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soil compactors are heavy machines used to compress soil and increase its density for construction purposes. The different types of soil compactors are Static Rolling Compactors, which use static pressure to compact soil; Impact Compactors, which use dynamic force to compress soil through impact; Vibrating Compactors, which use vibration to settle soil particles; and Compound Action Compactors, which combine different methods such as vibration and impact for more effective compaction. Each type of compactor is suited for different soil types and project requirements.</p></p>
