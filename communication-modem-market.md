<p><p>Communication modems are available in three different market types based on their communication capabilities. Duplex communication modems allow simultaneous two-way communication, while half-duplex communication modems enable communication in both directions but not simultaneously. Each market type caters to different communication requirements and constraints, offering a range of options for users depending on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
