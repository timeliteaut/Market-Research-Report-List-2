<p><p>Electronic Access Control Systems are classified into different types based on the method of authentication. Card-Based systems use proximity cards, smart cards, or key fobs for granting access to individuals. Biometric systems use unique physical characteristics like fingerprints, iris scans, or facial recognition for authentication. Other types include keypad systems, wireless systems, and keyless entry systems. Each system has its own advantages and limitations, making it crucial for organizations to choose the right type based on their security requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
