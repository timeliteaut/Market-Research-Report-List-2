Hand crank hydraulic lifts require manual operation to lift the patient, providing control over the lifting process. Push lever lifts use a lever system for raising and lowering the patient, allowing for easy operation. Other types of hydraulic lifts may incorporate different mechanisms for lifting, such as foot pedal operation or electric controls. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different user preferences and needs in patient handling.</p></p>
