<p><p>Carbon steel heat exchangers are classified into different types based on their design and application. Shell & Tube Heat Exchangers consist of tubes mounted within a cylindrical shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer between two fluids. Plate Heat Exchangers use metal plates to transfer heat, providing a compact and cost-effective solution. Air Cooled Heat Exchangers use ambient air to cool fluids, making them ideal for outdoor or high-temperature environments. Other market types may include double pipe heat exchangers, finned tube heat exchangers, and spiral heat exchangers.</p></p>
