<p><p>The Embedded Fingerprint Module market includes three main types: Capacitive Fingerprint Module, Optical Fingerprint Module, and Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module. Capacitive modules use electrical current to detect fingerprint patterns, while optical modules use light to capture images of fingerprints. Ultrasonic modules use sound waves to create a detailed 3D image of the fingerprint. Each type offers different levels of security and accuracy, catering to various needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
