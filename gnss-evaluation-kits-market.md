<p><p>GNSS Evaluation Kits are essential tools for assessing the performance and functionality of Global Navigation Satellite System receivers. These kits come in two main types: Single Frequency Receiver Evaluation Kits and Dual Frequency Receiver Evaluation Kits. Single Frequency kits are designed for evaluating receivers that operate on a single GNSS frequency, while Dual Frequency kits are used for assessing receivers that utilize two frequencies for improved accuracy and reliability. These kits provide valuable insights into the capabilities and limitations of GNSS receivers, helping developers optimize their systems for various applications.</p></p>
