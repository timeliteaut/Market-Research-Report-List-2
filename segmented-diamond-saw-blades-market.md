<p><p>Segmented diamond saw blades are categorized into three main types based on their manufacturing methods: sintered, welded, and electroplated.</p><p>Sintered diamond saw blades are made by mixing diamond grit with metal powders and then sintering them together at high temperatures. Welded diamond saw blades are created by welding segments of diamond grit onto the blade's core. Electroplated diamond saw blades have a layer of diamond grit bonded onto the blade's surface through an electroplating process.</p><p>Each type of diamond saw blade is suited for different cutting applications based on their unique construction methods.</p></p>
