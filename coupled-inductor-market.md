<p><ul><li>Multilayer Type</li><li>Wire-winding Type</li><li>Thin-film Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coupled inductors are classified into different types based on their design and construction. The Multilayer Type features multiple layers of insulation between the windings, offering high inductance and low resistance. The Wire-winding Type uses a single wire wound around a magnetic core, providing high inductance and current handling capacity. The Thin-film Type utilizes a thin film of conductive material for the windings, offering precise and stable performance. Other types of coupled inductors may include variations in design or materials used for construction.</p></p>
