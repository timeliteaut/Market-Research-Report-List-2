<p><p>Casing hangers are used in the oil and gas industry to support and hang casings in the wellbore. There are two main types of casing hangers - slip-type and core shaft type. Slip-type casing hangers use slips to grip the casing, providing support and preventing movement. Core shaft type casing hangers utilize a central shaft to secure the casing in place. Both types of casing hangers play a crucial role in well completion and integrity, ensuring the casing is properly supported and sealed in the wellbore.</p></p>
