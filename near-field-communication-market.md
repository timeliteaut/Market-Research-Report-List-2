<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) market types include Card Emulation, Reader Emulation, and Peer-to-peer. Card Emulation allows smartphones to act as virtual contactless cards for secure payments. Reader Emulation enables smartphones to read information from NFC tags or contactless cards. Peer-to-peer allows two NFC-enabled devices to communicate with each other to exchange information or files. Each market type serves different purposes, ranging from enabling mobile payments to transferring data between devices seamlessly.</p></p>
